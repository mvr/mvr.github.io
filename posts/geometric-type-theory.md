I have a [new
post](https://topos.institute/blog/2026-06-15-geometric-type-theory-done-two-ways/)
on the Topos blog.

>  A topos can be specified by the geometric theory that it
>  classifies. Though the sequents of a theory are described formally
>  and syntactically, its interaction with the world of sets (through
>  set-indexed disjunctions and axiom schemas) is often a little
>  hand-wavy. In this post I describe two ways of doing this more
>  precisely using type theory.
